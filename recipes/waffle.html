Waffle

Description

Simple pantry ingredients mix up quickly in this easy batter that can be used right away or stored in the refrigerator for up to a week. Serve waffles hot with whipped cream and fresh fruit or with butter and maple syrup for either breakfast, brunch, or a snack.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking Spray

Procedure

  1. Make the batter: Whisk the eggs, then add the flour, milk, and oil. Whisk in the sugar. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  2. Make the waffles: Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter.
